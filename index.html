Who We Are

Built to Close the Gap Between Skills & Income

WageTech (Women and Girls in Tech Excelling) is a transformative initiative designed to equip Nigerian women with digital skills and directly connect them to income opportunities.

We believe that learning alone is not enough — women must earn, grow, and lead in the digital economy.

🌟 Our Vision

A Nigeria where every woman has equal access to digital opportunities, income, and economic independence.

🎯 Our Mission

To create a sustainable pipeline from training → employment → entrepreneurship for women in tech.

Our Core Belief A sustainable pipeline: Training → Employment → Entrepreneurship
The Challenge

Nigeria's Digital Economy Is Booming — But Women Are Being Left Out

70%

Only 20–30% of trained women land paid opportunities. Thousands of Nigerian women acquire digital skills annually, yet the vast majority remain trapped between learning and earning — never breaking into the income they deserve.

WageTech exists to close this gap.
